Google calendar settingsTM

To setup a plan with its dedicated Google CalendarTM, an administrator must follow these steps:

  • Open up a GoogleTM account

  • Go to calendar settings and

    • grab the calendar's private key

    • grab the calendar's embed URL (up to the '?' character)

  • Grab the calendar's private key

  • Fill in the calendar configuration fields in the Administration page
