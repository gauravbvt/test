Information dissemination analysis panel

If planners systematically indicate how elements of information flowing out of a task relate to elements of information flowing into a task, Channels becomes able to track the planned dissemination of individual elements of information. Dissemination tracking can be done forward (where does an element of information go?) and backward (where does an element of information come from?).

A dissemination panel can be opened from a task, offering a choice of all elements received or sent by the task, or it can be opened from a flow, restricting the choice to the elements shared via the flow.

Dissemination tracking from a task gives the option of looking at the sources as well as at the targets of individual elements of information. A dissemination panel opened on a "receive" flow only tracks sources and a dissemination panel opened on a "send" flow only tracks targets.
