Tracking planned information dissemination

If planners systematically identify the non-implied relationships between received and sent elements of informations, then Channels can track the planned dissemination of individual elements of information. Dissemination tracking can be done forward (where does the element go?) and backward (where does the element come from?).

Dissemination tracking can be accessed from a part (offering a choice of all elements received or sent by the task) or it can be accessed from a flow (restricting the choice to the elements to those in the flow). Dissemination tracking from a part gives the option of looking at sources as wel as targets of individual elements of information. A receive flow only tracks sources and a send fow only tracks targets.

Dissemination tracking is visualized as element flows and also presented in table format, allowing sorting on various properties of a dissemination.
