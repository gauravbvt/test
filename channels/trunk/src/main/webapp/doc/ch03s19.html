Help, support and feedback

A planner accesses this help document by pressing on the help button.

The feedback button appearing next to the flow map in the Plan editor is meant to provide feedback about the Plan.

The feedback button appearing with the online help is meant to provide feedback about Channels itself.

The feedback button gives access to an online community support service (provided by BetaEasy TM). The service allows the planner to request a feature, report a bug or leave a comment. The planner can also browse the input of other planners.
