Channels is a collaborative Web application for building, analyzing and deploying Information Sharing Plans.

Each Information Sharing Plan has one development version, possibly one production version, and zero or more retired versions (prior production versions).

User roles

Channels recognizes three user roles: administrators, planners and Plan participants. Administrators assign planners and participants to plans hosted by a Channels instance. Planners jointly develop, review and deploy information sharing plans. Participants view their Procedures and respond to surveys from planners about issue resolutions.

An administrator can:

  • add new plans to Channels,

  • assign users to plans and change their user roles,

  • do what any planner or participant can do, but for all Plans.

A planner can:

  • view assigned plans (all versions),

  • modify the development version of an assigned Plan,

  • view the reports from an assigned Plan (all versions),

  • view all Procedures from an assigned Plan (all versions),

  • vote with other planners to put the development version of an assigned Plan into production,

  • launch, access and close online surveys of selected planners and participants about issues in an assigned Plan.

A participant can:

  • view the Procedures he/she is identified with from the production version of a Plan,

  • access and respond to online surveys.
