IS guidelines page

Channels produces on demand personalized information sharing guidelines in report format for each participant.

A participant accesses his/her IS guidelines directly from the home page. A planner accesses the IS guidelines of a participant (or an agent) indirectly from the participants list which is accessible from the home page.

The IS guidelines report organizes a participant's IS procedures generated from the plan by situations (relevant event phases possibly in the context of past or present events) and connects the IS procedures with related contact information. The IS guidelines are presented in a single HTML document suitable for browsing and printing.
