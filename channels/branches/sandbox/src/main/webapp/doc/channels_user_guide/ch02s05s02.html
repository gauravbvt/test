External services

Channels relies on a number of external, online services:

  • Geonames and Google MapsTM for geolocation

  • SurveyGizmosTM for launching surveys and collecting the results

  • An email service (SMTP) to send emails

  • Google CalendarTM to communicate calendar events of interest to users

Access to an external service requires account identification, either in the form of user id and password or in the form of an API key. When Channels is used as a service, external service configurations are already taken care of. However when installing a Channels server, an Administrator must acquire access to these external services and configure Channels to use the correct account information.

Planners need to log into the SurveyGizmoTM survey service to browse collected data and get reports. Planners also need to log into Google CalendarTM to add, edit or remove calendar events communicated in Channels.
