Information needs report

Channels produces, on demand, a report on the information needs of each participant.

A participant accesses his/her information needs report directly from the Home page. A planner accesses the information needs report of a participant (or an agent) indirectly from the participants list which is accessible from the Home page.

Figure 2.154. Accessing the information needs report of participants


The report lists the information needed by an agent to execute his/her assigned tasks in an IS plan. The information needs are organized by the tasks that require the information, and the tasks are themselves organized by the situations (relevant event phases, possibly in the context of past or present events) in which they are executed.

The degree to which information needs are satisfied (fully, somewhat or not at all) is indicated. The analysis is done at the level of individual elements of information. The information needs report is a single HTML document suitable for browsing and printing.
