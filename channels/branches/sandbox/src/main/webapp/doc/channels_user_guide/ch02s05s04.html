Google calendar settings

To setup a plan with its dedicated Google Calendar, an administrator must follow these steps:

  • Open up a Google account

  • Go to calendar settings and

    • grab the calendar's private key

    • grab the calendar's embedded URL (up to the '?' character)

  • Fill in the calendar configuration fields in the Administration page
