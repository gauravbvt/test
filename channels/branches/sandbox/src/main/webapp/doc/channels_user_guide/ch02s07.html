Assignments and Commitments Page

The assignments and commitments page enables planners to explore the task assignments and information sharing commitments (IS procedures) computed by Channels for a plan.

A planner first enters focus and summarization parameters for the generation of the visualization of tasks assignments and information sharing commitments. A planner then requests the visualization.

The focus parameters are:

  • A choice of viewing the IS procedures generated from one segment or from the entire plan

  • The identity of the agent or organization (taken as an aggregate of agents) for which to generate IS procedures

The summarization parameter determines how to aggregate, if at all, the task assignments of those other than the focused-on agent or organization. The choices are:

  • By organization - aggregate task assignments by organizations

  • By organization and role- aggregate task assignments by roles in organizations

  • By type of organization- aggregate task assignments by type of organization

  • By type of organization and role- aggregate task assignments by roles in types of organization

  • Don't summarize - show the task assignments of individual agents

Summarizing by type of organization and for a single segment minimizes the size of the visualization. Requesting to see the procedures for the entire plan and without summarization can yield very large visualizations that might take a while to display.

A planner can also request a drill-down report view of all (and only) the task assignments and sharing commitments shown in the visualization. At the top level of the report, all triggers for assigned tasks are listed and organized by events, by phases, and by how they are triggered (notification, request, automatically). The lists are initially collapsed and can be expanded.

A planner clicks on the trigger of an assigned task to open a view on the task. The Task view lists the information received and sent by the task's assignees.
