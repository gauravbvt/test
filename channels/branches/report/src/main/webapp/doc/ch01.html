Why plan information sharing

Organizations operate in an increasingly inter-connected and inter-dependent world. Systemic risks are growing. Organizations must network to compete in a global marketplace. They can no longer respond to major crisis nor can they seize global business opportunities on their own. Success now largely depends on the timely and targeted sharing of information within and between organizations.

During a crisis, information will not spontaneously flow as needed. Maintaining contact lists is not enough; organizations must plan ahead and create actionable information sharing procedures.

Information Sharing Planning provides a framework and method for designing and improving the information sharing procedures required to successfully manage systemic risks and seize opportunities.
